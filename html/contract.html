Between Blood Donation Management System and the Donor/Blood Bank

Effective Date:

Important Notice

Please read this agreement carefully before using our services. By registering an account, you agree to be bound by the terms and conditions set forth below.

1. Parties to the Agreement

1.1

This Agreement is between Blood Donation Management System (hereinafter referred to as "BDMS") and the User (either a Blood Donor or Blood Bank) who registers an account on our platform.

2. Services Provided

2.1

BDMS provides a platform to connect blood donors with those in need of blood donations and facilitates the management of blood bank inventories.

2.2

BDMS does not directly handle blood products but serves as an informational platform to connect parties.

3. User Responsibilities

3.1

Users must provide accurate and truthful information during registration and when using the platform.

3.2

Blood Donors must meet all eligibility requirements for blood donation as per their local health regulations.

3.3

Blood Banks must maintain accurate and up-to-date information about blood inventory and availability.

4. Privacy and Data Protection

4.1

BDMS collects and processes personal data in accordance with our Privacy Policy.

4.2

User data will not be shared with third parties without explicit consent, except as required by law.

5. Limitation of Liability

5.1

BDMS is not liable for any health complications that may arise from blood donation or transfusion.

5.2

BDMS does not guarantee the accuracy of information provided by users on the platform.

6. Termination

6.1

Either party may terminate this agreement by providing written notice to the other party.

6.2

BDMS reserves the right to suspend or terminate accounts that violate the terms of this agreement.
